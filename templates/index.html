Who is DonorsChoose.org?

DonorsChoose.org is a US-based nonprofit organization that allows individuals to donate money directly to public school classroom projects. Public school teachers post classroom project requests on the website, allowing individuals and companies to browse through them and donate money directly to a project of their choice. The projects fund a variety of things, such as new classroom equipment, learning materials and school trips.

DonorsChoose.org was founded in 2000 by a history teacher working in a Bronx public high school. Since inception it has reached over 70% of public schools across the US, raising hundreds of millions of dollars in funding.

DonorsChoose.org has made its data available to the public for making discoveries and building applications.


What is the purpose of this website?

I am a graduate who benefited from DonorsChoose.org’s funding whilst at high school. I have built this website to promote the good work that DonorsChoose.org does, and as an interactive tool to allow people to look more closely at the projects supported.


How do I use this website?

The data provided by DonorsChoose.org has been used to build interactive visualisations that you can manipulate to show information that interests you. For instance, you can look at data from your home state, or for projects supporting your favourite subject, or both at the same time!

There are instructions written to help you manipulate the visualisations in order to filter the information you are looking for. Just click on the ‘Start Tour!’ button at the top of the page to begin!
